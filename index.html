Who I Am

About Me

20+ Years in Leadership

Namaste! I am Narendra Modi, born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat. From humble beginnings — helping my father sell tea at a railway station — I learned the values of hard work and perseverance that have guided my entire life.

I dedicated my early years to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), which shaped my commitment to national service. I served as Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014, transforming it into one of India's most developed states.

Since 2014, as Prime Minister, I have been working tirelessly to build a Viksit Bharat — a developed, self-reliant, and inclusive India that leads the world with pride.

Journey

Political Timeline

1950

Born in Vadnagar, Gujarat

Born into a humble family, helped father sell tea at railway station.

1971

Joined RSS Full-Time

Dedicated life to national service through the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh.

1987

Joined BJP

Became an active member of the Bharatiya Janata Party, rising through the ranks.

2001

Chief Minister of Gujarat

Became CM of Gujarat, transforming it into India's most developed state.

2014

Prime Minister – 1st Term

Led BJP to historic majority, became 14th PM of India with 282 seats.

2019

Prime Minister – 2nd Term

Re-elected with even larger mandate of 303 seats. Article 370 abrogated.

2024

Prime Minister – 3rd Term

Sworn in for historic 3rd consecutive term, continuing Viksit Bharat mission.
